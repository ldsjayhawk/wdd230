Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Overview

Site Name

Overland Park Chamber of Commerce. I chose Overland Park because this is the city that I live in and it provides a familiar place.

Site Purpose

The Overland Park Chamber of Commerce connects businesses together to work together to make a stronger community. The Chamber of Commerce also provides connections to customers within the community.

Scenarios

  • How do I find a business to help me with a need?
  • What events are being held that can help connect me with other businesses or customers?
  • How do I join the Chamber of Commerce?
